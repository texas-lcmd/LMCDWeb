About Longhorns Crushing Medical Debt

Our fight against medical debt in partnership with Dollar For

$ 39,742,140

IN MEDICAL DEBT RELIEF NATION WIDE

$ 1,200,000

IN MEDICAL DEBT RELIEF IN AUSTIN

Dollar For is a national non-profit organization that eliminates medical debt by empowering patients and advocating on their behalf. Their biggest effort is expressed in their Debt Forgiveness program, in which they enforce the hospital's Charity Care policies. In order for hospitals to keep their nonprofit status, they have to have programs in place that aid families without incomes large enough to cover their medical expenses, called Charity Care. Using their resources, we first check a patient's eligibility. Dollar For then works through a more extensive application with the patient and reach out to the hospitals, advocating for the family and reinforcing that the hospital waives the debt it is legally responsible for.

At UT Austin, our goal is to form a group of passionate students into an independent UT chapter, mobilized towards eliminating medical debt and helping people navigate the healthcare financial system after a medical crisis!
