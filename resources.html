Member Points

To qualify for active general membership you must:

  • Attend biweekly General Meetings
  • Collect 4 volunteering points
  • Collect 3 social points

To qualify for leadership positions you must:

  • Attend biweekly General Meetings
  • Collect 8 volunteering points
  • Collect 6 social points

Want to become a Volunteer Ambassador?

Ambassadors get the word out to the community about Dollar For's programs and charity care. Dollar For can only help patients receive financial assistance if they are aware of the services that are offered.

If you are interested in signing up to become a Volunteer Ambassador please complete the following:

  1. Click Here and check the Ambassador Box so we can have your e-mail on file. You will then receive an email with all the tools you need to get started!
  2. Download our Key Talking Points to familiarize yourself with Dollar For's mission and services. This will prepare you to speak to potential clients and to promote Dollar For in the near future!
  3. See our Public Talking Scripts if you want more detailed responses on any questions that might arise during tabling/outreach events.
  4. View additional Spanish Resources to provide more information when doing outreach in communities with Spanish-speaking individuals.
